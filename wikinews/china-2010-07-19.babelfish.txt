Chinese government tries to contain petroleum spill in Yellow Sea 
19 of 2010 July 
The Government of China sent considering of 800 boats to try to contain crude the petroleum spill happened in two pipe lines property of semi-official China National Petroleum Corporation, near the harbor city of Dalian, the northwest of this country, and one of most important. 
Firemen took near 15 hours in extinguishing the fire happened after an explosion in two pipe lines, which brought about the spill of 1500 tons of crude in an area of 183 kilometers squared of the sea of Bohai. Near 80 boats they realised workings of cleaning and containment of the power one. 
Authorities considered that the cleaning workings will take five days more, while million petroleum barrels are turned aside towards other facilities. The accident happened to about 100 kilometers of the coast and caused the closing of the port of Dalian. The industrial complex is one of four greater of China, and esteem that has reserves of 19 million barrels.
